Since 2024, I have been an ATER (teaching and research assistant) in applied mathematics at the University of Perpignan Via Domitia (UPVD). My work focuses on the numerical analysis of partial differential equations (PDEs), with a special interest in finite element methods, interface problems and a posteriori error estimation.

During my PhD at the University of Pau and Pays de l’Adour (UPPA), within the Laboratoire de Mathématiques et de leurs Applications (LMAP), I studied conservative flux reconstruction techniques and developed guaranteed a posteriori error estimators for elliptic interface problems, including unfitted discretizations such as CutFEM.

My research lies at the interface between rigorous analysis, numerical methods, and scientific computing, with applications to heterogeneous media and complex geometries.
