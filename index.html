About

I am an ATER in applied mathematics at the University of Perpignan. My research focuses on numerical analysis of PDEs, finite element methods, flux reconstruction, and a posteriori error estimation for elliptic interface problems, with a particular interest in CutFEM and unfitted meshes.

Research interests

  • A posteriori error analysis and adaptive mesh refinement
  • Conservative flux reconstruction for elliptic problems
  • Finite element methods (conforming / nonconforming / mixed)
  • Interface problems and CutFEM / unfitted methods
